2. Health and Lifestyle

Habits + Time Management

A huge part of my focus in life has been habits and time management. After reading Atomic Habits and 7 Habits of Effective People, I realized that I needed to change my lifestyle if I wanted to become who I wish to be. I have been developing several habits such as daily workouts, scheduling my time, reading, saying my prayers, brushing twice a day, learning something new everyday, and waking up early. I keep count of everyday that I have completed these habits and by using "streaks", I motivate myself to continue with this self-improvement habits. 

Food + Nutrition

I have had uncontrolled overeating habits and a sweet tooth for most of my life. This has led me to an unhealthy lifestyle and body. I realized this in grade 11 and began to try to improve. After reading Atomic Habits and reflecting, I realized the subconscious motive was my urge to eat until I felt stuffed. I started using little tricks like eating from smaller plates and chewing for longer times. After a while, these tricks changed my subconscious to think I was stuffed. It took a few years to achieve a healthy weight, but I am proud of and love who I am now.



Sports + Fitness

Being overweight and unfit has been an issue for the past 10 years of my life. I have been bullied and lost my self-esteem because of it. In the past few years, I realized that I needed to be healthier
for a longer life. It was difficult to achieve due to constantly changing environments and uncontrolled overeating habits. One lesson I learned was consistency is key for fitness. My ever-changing environment would constantly break my momentum in workouts. I would lose the motivation to get back on the right track. Recently,
I overcame this challenge by developing workouts which I could do in 15 minutes, no matter where I was.  By creating an adaptable system which was easy to do every day, I built up my momentum again and have been working out daily for over 100 days.
